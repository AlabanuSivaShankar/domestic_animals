About us

Who we are?

At DomesticGuardians, we are dedicated to the welfare and protection of domestic animals worldwide. Our mission is to raise awareness, offer resources for care and protection, and take direct action to prevent cruelty and neglect toward vulnerable domestic animals. With over a decade of experience, our team of veterinarians, animal behaviorists, and compassionate volunteers work tirelessly to ensure the well-being of household pets and farm animals, safeguarding them and promoting responsible, ethical care practices.

projects

What we did?

Project Safe Paws A dedicated effort to protect stray dogs and cats in urban areas, focusing on rescuing and rehoming animals at risk of abuse and neglect.

Project Farmstead Haven – A committed initiative to safeguard farm animals, such as cows, chickens, and goats, from inhumane treatment, promoting ethical practices and improved living conditions in rural communities.

Project Neighborhood Watch – An effort to protect domestic pets from abandonment and illegal trade, providing resources to reunite lost pets with their owners and prevent pet trafficking.
